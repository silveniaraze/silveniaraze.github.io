S.Coups

S.Coups (Korean: 에스쿱스), born on August 8, 1995, as Choi Seungcheol (Korean: 최승철), is a South Korean rapper under Pledis Entertainment. He is the leader of the boy group SEVENTEEN and its Hip-Hop Team sub-unit. He is also the leader of the special sub-unit SVT LEADERS.

Jeonghan

Jeonghan (Korean: 정한), born as Yoon Jeonghan (Korean: 윤정한), is a South Korean singer and performer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the South Korean boy group SEVENTEEN under 'Vocal Team'.

Joshua

Joshua Hong (Korean name: Hong Jisoo; in Hangul: 홍지수) referred to as JOSHUA (Hangul: 조슈아), is a Korean-American singer and performer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN under "Vocal Team".

Jun

Jun (Korean: 준), born on June 10, 1996, as Wen Junhui (Chinese: 文俊辉), is a Chinese singer, dancer, and actor under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the South Korean boy group SEVENTEEN under the Performance Team.

Hoshi

Hoshi (Korean: 호시), born as Kwon Soonyoung (Korean: 권순영), is a South Korean performer and choreographer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN and the leader of the Performance Team. He is also a member of the special sub-units BSS and SVT LEADERS.

Wonwoo

Wonwoo (Korean: 원우), born as Jeon Wonwoo (Korean: 전원우), is a South Korean rapper under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN under the Hip-Hop Team.

Woozi

Woozi (Korean: 우지), born as Lee Jihoon (Korean: 이지훈), is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and producer under Pledis Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group SEVENTEEN and the leader of Vocal Team. He is also part of the special sub-unit, 'Leaders'.

DK
